Go Skateboarding Day June 21st!

Come out and support our local skateboard community Monday, June 21st. All ages are welcome to attend. The event will be held downtown between 12pm-8pm in our beautiful city of Memphis, TN. You can expect a large crowd in attencance for this fun filled day. There will be food trucks, vendors, contests, and much more at this event as well. Please invite as many friends and family as you would like. SPREAD THE WORD!
